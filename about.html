About Us

We turn sugarcane waste into black gold by converting residues into biochar, a soil-enriching product that boosts yields and combats climate change.

Our project operates in Kakamega, Mumias, and Muhoroni, addressing environmental challenges while creating economic opportunities for smallholder farmers and cooperatives.
