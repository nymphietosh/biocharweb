Turning Sugarcane Waste into Black Gold

In Kakamega, Mumias, and Muhoroni, sugarcane residues are often burned, releasing harmful emissions and degrading soil. Our solution? Converting this waste into biochar – a soil-enriching carbon product that boosts farm yields, fights climate change, and turns agricultural pollution into profit.

Fight Climate Change

Sequester carbon in soil for decades, reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

Turn Waste into Profit

Convert sugarcane residues into valuable biochar and premium soil blends.
