Market & Impact

Our project focuses on transforming sugarcane waste into valuable biochar products that support sustainable farming and climate action in Western Kenya.

Target Markets include smallholder farmers, sugarcane cooperatives, organic farms, agro-dealers, and NGOs dedicated to soil health and climate adaptation.

We generate revenue through direct biochar sales, premium compost blends, carbon credit partnerships, waste management fees, and paid training collaborations.

By diverting harmful sugarcane residues from burning and converting them into soil-enriching biochar, we reduce emissions, improve soil fertility, and create new income streams for local communities.
